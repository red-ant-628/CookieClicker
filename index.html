About Cookie Clicker


This is a fun game for people who just want to keep clicking a cookie over and over again. It may be simple, but it has many uses:

  • Challenge your friends to a cookie duel and see who can click the most cookies in a minute!
  • Use the cookie counter as a tally for the wins you have in a game of rock-paper-scissors!
  • Or, simply enjoy the relaxing action of repeatedly clicking a cookie!

It's up to you.
